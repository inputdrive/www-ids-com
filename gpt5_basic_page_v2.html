A Practical Starter Template for an AdSense‑Ready Site

By Alex Example • Updated September 1, 2025

What makes a page acceptable?

Google looks for original, valuable content, a good user experience, and compliance with policies. This template keeps layout shifts low, loads fast, and includes clear navigation and site policies.

Avoid intrusive UX patterns: auto‑refreshing pages, misleading labels, or placing ads where users are likely to mis‑tap. Keep ad density reasonable—especially on mobile—and don’t hide content behind ads.

Performance & UX basics

  • Fast load times (light CSS, no heavy frameworks).
  • Accessible markup (labels, landmarks, good contrast).
  • Responsive, mobile‑first layout.
  • Clear navigation and policy pages/sections.

Content quality

Publish original writing, add images you own or have rights to use, and avoid scraped or auto‑generated text. Keep topics safe for ads: no adult, illegal, hateful, or shocking content.
